About Us

Our Story

Founded with a passion for service and a commitment to humanity, Emergency Relief Supplies Limited (ERS) has grown to become a cornerstone in the logistics and relief sector in Kenya.

We understand the critical nature of humanitarian work. Every delay can cost lives, which is why we have built a robust network and infrastructure to ensure that help reaches where it is needed, when it is needed.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, reliable, and timely logistics and relief supply solutions that empower humanitarian efforts and improve lives.

Our Vision

To be the leading partner in humanitarian logistics in East Africa, known for integrity, professionalism, and operational excellence.

Environmental Policy

At ERS, we are committed to sustainable practices. We strive to minimize our carbon footprint through efficient route planning, eco-friendly packaging where possible, and responsible waste management in our warehouses.
